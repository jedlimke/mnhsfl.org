The MNHSFL is hosting a referee certification clinic on December 15th for current and aspiring referees.

This clinic is perfect for fencers looking to give back to the community, parents interested in learning more about the sport, or anyone who wants to become a certified referee.

**Clinic Details:**
- Date: December 15, 2025
- Time: 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- Location: Downtown Fencing Club
- Cost: $50 (includes materials and lunch)

Participants will receive certification upon successful completion of the course. No prior refereeing experience required!

Space is limited. Email info@mnhsfl.org to register.
